The briards,or berger de brie,is a large breed of dog traditionally used a herder and garden of sheap.

Breed History

The Briards,which is beleived to have an originated in France,has been breed for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries,messengers and search for wouded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing.Briards were used in First World War almost to the point of extinction.Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering.Chariemagne,Napolean,Thomas Jefferson and Lafayatte all owned briards.
