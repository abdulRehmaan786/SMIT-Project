Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 table spoon butter.
  • 2 table spoon cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter is melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the egg from the edge of the pan to the center over 20 seconds (as if you are making omelate).
  5. When the egg still moist remove from the heat (it will continue on the plate until served).
